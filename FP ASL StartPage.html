ASL Fingerspelling Quiz

  • Enter words into the box below.
  • Your added words will be shown below the "Start" and "Reset" buttons.
  • When ready, click the "Start" button to randomly generate a word that will be handspelled back to you.
  • After the word has been handspelled, type your answer in the second box labeled "Submit answer".
  • If you are unsure of the answer click the "Replay button to have the word handspelled back to you again.
  • When you have correctly guessed the handspelled word, press the "Continue" button to move onto the next word.
  • You may click on the "Continue" when you are ready to move onto the next word, wether you correctly guessed the word or not.
